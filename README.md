# Daily Bible Reading App

A simple app for following daily Bible readings based on the **Christadelphian Bible Reading Companion** by Robert Roberts, with support for multiple Indian language Bibles.

## Features

- 📖 Daily Bible readings following the Christadelphian Bible Reading Companion
- 📚 Three daily reading portions
- 🌐 Support for multiple Indian language Bibles
- ✨ Simple and easy-to-use interface
- ✅ Helps maintain a consistent Bible reading routine

## About

This app is designed to help users stay consistent with their daily Bible reading journey by following the reading plan from the **Christadelphian Bible Reading Companion** by Robert Roberts.

The reading schedule divides each day into **three reading portions**, helping users read through the Bible systematically and develop a regular reading habit.

## Supported Languages

The app includes Bible support for multiple Indian languages, making daily reading more accessible for users who prefer reading Scripture in their native language.

## Purpose

The goal of this app is to provide a convenient and accessible way to follow a structured Bible reading plan every day.

## Inspiration

The reading plan used in this app is based on the **Christadelphian Bible Reading Companion** created by Robert Roberts.
